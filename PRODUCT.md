# BadalonaMar

## Register
product

## Product Purpose
BadalonaMar is a Catalan-only mobile web app for quickly understanding what is useful today around Canyado, Casagemes, Centre, and the nearby Badalona seafront.

## Users
Residents, regular visitors, and curious locals who check the app from a phone while commuting, walking near the sea, planning the day, or deciding what to read or visit.

## Tone
Local, direct, calm, and useful. The product should feel like a well-kept neighborhood noticeboard with live data, not a municipal portal and not a marketing page.

## Principles
- Daily usefulness first: weather, beach state, news, events, recommendations.
- Catalan only in v1.
- Focus on Canyado, Casagemes, Centre, and nearby sea-facing Badalona.
- Prefer official/public data sources and isolated server-side adapters.
- Keep interaction ready for later, but do not ship voting or auth in the MVP.
