# BadalonaMar Design

## Scene
A person checks the app outdoors on a bright phone screen near the Badalona seafront, often one-handed, wanting the day distilled in a few seconds.

## Theme
Light theme with tinted neutrals, sea-washed green and blue accents, and small coral moments for attention. Avoid dark dashboards, beach postcard cliches, gradients, and oversized marketing heroes.

## Color
Use OKLCH tokens in CSS. Neutrals are warm and lightly sea-tinted, never pure white or pure black. Accent color is restrained and used for navigation, status, and key calls to action.

## Typography
Use a product UI sans stack for speed and legibility. Headings are compact and confident; body copy stays short.

## Components
Cards are allowed for individual content items only. Sections should feel like full-width bands and lists, not nested panels. Buttons and navigation use familiar icon-led controls.
